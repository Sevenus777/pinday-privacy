Privacy Policy for PinDay

Last updated: 2026-04-23

PinDay (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides a habit tracking and accountability application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use PinDay.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and display name
  • User-generated content, such as tasks, reminders, progress records, and weight records
  • Friend/accountability information, such as friend connections and selected shared task visibility
  • Technical information necessary to operate and secure the service

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To sync your tasks, progress, and related app data across sessions
  • To support social/accountability features such as friend visibility and friend requests
  • To improve app reliability, performance, and security
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only as needed to operate the service, including with infrastructure or hosting providers. Information you choose to share with friends through app features will be visible to those friends.

4. Data Storage and Retention

We retain your data for as long as needed to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

5. Your Choices

  • You can update certain profile information inside the app
  • You can control which tasks are visible to friends
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data

6. Children’s Privacy

PinDay is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
